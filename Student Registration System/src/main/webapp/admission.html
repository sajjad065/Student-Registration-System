Admission Requirements

The following are the list that are needed for admission to the university.

  • Online Application Form
  • Transcript from Recently Graduate School
  • Letters of Recommendation
  • Standardized Test Scores (SAT or ACT)
  • Standardized Test Scores (GRE or GMAT for Grad only)
  • English Proficiency Test for International Student (Ielts or Toefl)
  • Statement of Purpose
  • Letter of Sponsorships
